About the SpaceX Project

SpaceX is a pioneer in the aerospace industry with a series of successful launches and innovative missions.

Their achievements include missions such as Demo Mission 2, the Falcon Heavy test flight and the Inspiration4 mission.

The company has developed advanced technologies, such as reusable rockets and Starlink satellite constellations for the global internet.
